Moira Lang

Moira Lang grew out of equal parts commercial instinct, queer spunk and ache, class friction, and a long, intimate education in Philippine cinema.

She has co-written films including Anak, Tanging Yaman, Kailangan Kita, and Milan.

As a producer she continued pushing difficult and emotionally charged cinema across mainstream and independent film.

Michiko Yamamoto

Michiko Yamamoto writes ordinary people cornered by systems larger than themselves: family, poverty, institutions, masculinity, and history.

Her work includes Magnifico, Ang Pagdadalaga ni Maximo Oliveros, On the Job, Honor Thy Father, and Manila's Finest.

Her screenplays move between tenderness and violence without announcing the shift.
